# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, welcome constructive feedback, focus criticism on ideas and code, and avoid harassment or discriminatory behavior.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainer through [GitHub's abuse-reporting tools](https://support.github.com/contact/report-abuse). Reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when conduct harms the community.

This policy applies in project repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, and other spaces where someone represents the project.
